WHAT ARE THE SYMPTOMS OF COVID-19

SYMPTOMS OF CORONAVIRUS

Fever

High Fever-this means you feel hot and to touch on your chest or back (you do not measure your temperature ) its a common sign and also may appear in 2-10 days if you attected

Cough

Continuous cough-this means coughing a lot for more than an hour , or 3 or more coughing episodes in 24 hour (if you usally have a cough , it may be worse than usual)

Shortness of breath

Diffculty breathing Around 1 out of every 6 people who gets COVID-19 becomes seriously ill and develops Diffculty breathing or Shortness of breath

Stay at home and call your doctor: if you have been esprded to COVID-19 and develop a fever and any Symptoms , such as cough or Diffculty breathing , call your healthycare provider as soon as possible for medical advices
